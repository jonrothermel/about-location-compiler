ABOUT

Location Compiler is a database of film locations in New York City that are browsable by neighborhoods in each of the five boroughs. To use the site, click on a borough to view neighborhoods in that borough. Then, click on the neighborhood that you want to look at to see a pop-up with all the databases locations in that pop-up.

Location Compiler NYC was made by Jonathan Rothermel in his web development class at The King’s College. He made it with the help of his teacher, Taq, in the fall semester of 2017. The website is built using custom code with by Bootstrap and Stephan Wagner’s jBox.
